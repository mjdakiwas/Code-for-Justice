About Us

We are a nonprofit made up of problem solvers. The problem? We have the world in the palms of our hands- at least some of us do. But websites and software tend to be geared toward those of us who have excellent vision, great memory, good hearing, two hands, and perfect dexterity. That means that the internet is exclusive. We work with brands and business owners to make their digital presence more inclusive. We rework their websites and apps with accessibility in mind, and we do it for free. Our goal is to create a digital world where there is compassion built into the code. Our funding comes from our sponsors, who continuously buy into our vision because they believe in the cause as much as we do. With this funding, we can educate a new generation of software engineers who are aware of the accessibility gap and work to close it. Some of these engineers go on to work at large corporations where they can make meaningful changes to systems that affect our everyday lives. We also educate small business owners who may not have the budget to employ an entire department focused on accessibility, because the internet is better when we all have access.
